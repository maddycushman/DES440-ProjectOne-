'80

Pac-Man

IT ALL STARTED WITH PIZZA...

To call Pac-Man a mere game would be an injustice. It’s a cultural icon and has continued to be loved for generations. Like any good idea, it started with pizza. Toru Iwatani claims that he was staring at a pizza when he had the idea for a video game centered around eating. Today, the game is cited as the highest grossing video game of all time.

The Shining

REDRUM, REDRUM, REDRUM

The iconic horror film directed by Stanley Kubrick ended up being a horror on and off the screen. The movie is about supernatural forces putting pressure on an already fractured family. There was claustrophobia and agoraphobia on and off the screen as Kubrick made the cast feel just as crazy as the characters they were playing. If fact, it was so strenuous, Shelley Duvall became physically ill from the stress of shooting.
